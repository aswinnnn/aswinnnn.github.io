My Dissatisfaction


17 August 2022 (Wednesday) -

My realization was a good note to leave my blog on and never write again till yet another realization led me to believe it was only half the battle.
I've only been comfortable enough with the people around me and the places around me but I haven't been comfortable in my own mind yet. I'd blame this on my social anxiety but you know what? I blame it on my entire upbringing.
While being "social" I still have to suffer through the ideas of envy, jealousy, greed and other biblical offenses but practical daily thoughts you'd have to go through at school. It's okay that I go through this and Its more than okay that I am realizing it and that I want to change how I behave and be more comfortable in my own mind.
I love everyone around me but I cannot have everyone around me.
Is what I have better than what I lack? - the question itself doesn't make much sense but the more and more I look into it the more profound effect it has on me.
Its not all "me vs me" either, I know a few pretty good people now and I got to know like one of the coolest people I'd ever meet in this school today. Never give up hope, people. That's all for today.
